SUBC Submarine Drivetrain

1 / 3

Description: Designed and manufactured the human-powered drivetrain for the 25/26 competition submarine. In particular, I was in charge of concept generation, optimization, and manufacturing of the side plates and step-down collar. I modelled the components in SolidWorks, performed FEA-driven optimization, and worked with a machinist to CNC the components. This work reduced the gearbox's weight by 50% and completely eliminate output gear slippage.

What I Learned: Mechanical design, power transmission, CNC, and manufacturing techniques.

Technologies: Solidworks, CNC, 3D Printing, FEA
